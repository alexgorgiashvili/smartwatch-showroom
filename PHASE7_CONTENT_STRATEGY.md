## 🔗 Internal Linking Strategy

### **Hub & Spoke Model:**

**Hub Pages (Main Landing):**
1. Homepage → Links to all categories
2. Products Index → Links to individual products
3. Blog Index → Links to all articles
4. City Pages → Links to products + guides

**Spoke Pages (Supporting Content):**
- Product pages → Link to guides, reviews, related products
- Blog posts → Link to products, other articles, guides
- Guides → Link to products, blog posts

### **Linking Rules:**

1. **Product Pages:**
   - Minimum 3 internal links
   - Link to: Related products, SIM guide, Gift guide
   - Use descriptive anchor text

2. **Blog Posts:**
   - Minimum 5 internal links
   - Link to: 2-3 products, 2-3 related articles
   - Natural anchor text

3. **Landing Pages:**
   - Link to: 4-6 products, 2-3 guides
   - City-specific content

### **Anchor Text Variations:**

**Product Links:**
- Exact match: "ბავშვის სმარტ საათი GPS-ით"
- Partial match: "სმარტ საათი GPS ტრეკინგით"
- Branded: "MyTechnic GPS საათი"
- Generic: "იხილეთ პროდუქტი"

**Article Links:**
- Descriptive: "როგორ ავირჩიოთ სმარტ საათი"
- Question-based: "რა არის GPS ტრეკინგი?"
- Action: "წაიკითხეთ სრული სახელმძღვანელო"

## 📊 Content Performance Metrics

### **Track for Each Article:**

1. **Organic Traffic:** Sessions from Google
2. **Engagement:** Time on page, bounce rate
3. **Conversions:** Product clicks, purchases
4. **Rankings:** Position for target keywords
5. **Backlinks:** External links earned

### **Success Criteria:**

- **Traffic:** 500+ monthly visits per article (after 3 months)
- **Engagement:** >2 min average time on page
- **Conversion:** >5% click-through to products
- **Rankings:** Top 10 for primary keyword
- **Backlinks:** 2+ quality backlinks per article
